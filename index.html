Bourbon/Whiskey Cocktails

  • Old Fashioned

    • The old fasioned is a true timeless cocktail to cause the drinker to remember the days when men like Don Draper had the run of the town.

    Traditionally made by muddling sugar with bitters, then adding the alcohol - traditionally whiskey or bourbon.

    A twist of orange rind, and presentation over a single ice cube in a short tumber finishes off this classic of a cocktail.

  • Manhattan

    • Bourbon is a traditionally southern spirit, but this timeless drink was created in the northern city of New York City.

    3 parts Bourbon and 1 part sweet Vermouth are mixed with a few light dashes of bitters.

    Served either up in a martini glass or over ice in a rocks glass, the final touch is a garnish of a Maraschino Cherry.

  • Bourbon Sour

    • A favorite of Johnny Depp, thsi simple Bourbon cocktail will pack a punch while delivering a refreshing cleansing of the palette.

    2 parts Bourbon mixed with 1 part Lemon Juice will deliver the traditional sour twist sought after by fans of this drink.

    Traditionally served over ice with a dash of sugar and two Maraschino Cherries counteract the sour taste.

    Vodka Cocktails

    • Vodka Martini

      • The ultimate classic drink, favorites of men such as James Bond, and all those who desire to emulate him.

      In the words of Jed Bartlet: Shaken, not stirred, will get you cold water with a dash of (gin) and dry vermouth.

      The reason you stir it with a special spoon is so not to chip the ice. James is ordering a weak martini and being snooty about it.

      Vermouth is a tricky addition to the martini, at least for those uninitiated in how to order a martini correctly.

      • "Dry" refers to the addition of only a splash of vermouth to lend a bitter taste to the drink.
      • "Extra Dry" will only have a tiny drizzle of vermouth, or none at all.
      • "Wet" martinis will have close to equal parts of vodka and vermouth.
      • A "Perfect" martini will have equal parts of dry and sweet vermouth mixed with the vodka.

      Ultimately the vodka martini is garnished with 3 olives on a toothpick. Blue Cheese stuffed olives are a favorite.

      The addition of olive juice to the traditional martini will create the "dirty" variation.
